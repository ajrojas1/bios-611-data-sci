Urban Ministries of Durham

Urban Ministries of Durham (UMD) is a non-profit organization that provides services for homeless families and individuals. In this project, I will use UMDs data to visualize the demographic information that makes up UMDs clients. Another component of this project will include the spatial distribution of race and ethnicity within Durham countys sub-divisions, so that this data may be compared to UMDs client data.

This project will use information recorded of UMDs clients ethnicity and race, as well as 3rd party demographic and spatial data to visualize Durhams countys demographic information.
